6 Campaign Analysis
Key insights from the campaign

📊 Participation & Eligibility

32 clients registered, but only 10 (31.3%) earned at least 1 ticket. 22 clients registered but failed to meet the tandem deposit + trading requirement. The biggest drop-off factor was trading volume — several clients deposited enough but didn't trade enough lots (e.g., Adrial Nanda Putra deposited $10,000 but only traded 1.12 lots).

🎫 Ticket Concentration

79 total tickets were held by 10 eligible clients. The top 4 clients (NURSAID: 18, Miftakhus Surur: 14, Puji Kris Widodo: 13, M RIZKIANTO RAMADHAN: 13) held 73.4% of all tickets (58 out of 79). This concentration is expected given the proportional mechanic.

🏆 Winner Distribution

24 prizes distributed across 7 unique winners (out of 10 eligible). 3 eligible clients (anthony rianto, MUSARISTON, Ribun Bunyamin via portal 643276) did not win — consistent with random probability given their low ticket counts (1, 1, and 2 respectively).

💰 Total Campaign Deposits

Eligible clients deposited a combined $31,233.34 USD in new net deposits during Q1 2026. The cost of 24× 0.5g gold bars (approx. $40–50 each retail) is roughly $960–$1,200 total, giving an estimated ROI of 26–32x on promotional spend vs. deposits generated.

👥 Account Manager Split

Christy H managed 22 clients (8 eligible, 68 tickets). Hendri C managed 10 clients (2 eligible, 18 tickets but concentrated in NURSAID alone). Future campaigns may benefit from more balanced AM coverage.

8 Ticket Calculation Logic
How tickets were calculated — based on T&C clauses 4a–4f

Core Formula (Clause 4b)

Tickets = min( floor( Net Deposit ÷ $300 ) , floor( Trading Lots ÷ 3 ) )

Both conditions must be met simultaneously — this is the tandem requirement. The lower of the two determines the ticket count.

Additional rules: Only positions held ≥2 minutes count. Hedging orders are excluded from volume calculation.

Per-Portal Rule (Clause 4c)

Tickets are calculated independently per portal. Deposits across portals are NOT aggregated. No pro-rata for amounts below $300.
